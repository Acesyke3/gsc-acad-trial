The ACE Framework — What They Learned

ACE框架——孩子今天掌握的核心工具

This is not a list of tips. It is a speaking architecture — a structural map the brain can trust under pressure. When students know exactly where they're going, hesitation disappears.

这不是技巧清单，而是一种表达架构——让大脑在高压时刻有路可循。当学生清楚地知道自己要去哪里，犹豫就会自然消失。

A

Anchor

开场锚定

Open with a hook that demands attention — a bold question, a surprising fact, or a short story. The Anchor earns the right to be heard.

用一句让人停下来的话开场：一个问题、一个反常识的事实、或一段小故事。开场决定了你是否值得被倾听。

C

Case

论证立场

Build the argument in three layers: a clear Point, a logical Reason, and a concrete Example. One strong chain — not a scattered list.

三层论证结构：论点 → 理由 → 具体例子。一条清晰的逻辑链，而非杂乱的罗列。

E

Echo

有力收尾

Land the speech with impact: callback to the opening, a call to action, or one unforgettable closing image. The Echo is what audiences remember.

有力地结尾：呼应开场、发出号召、或留下难忘的最后一句。这是听众记得最久的部分。
